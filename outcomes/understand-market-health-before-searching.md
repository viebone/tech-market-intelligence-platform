# Outcome: UX professionals can assess job market health before committing to a search

## Signal
Senior UX job search journey research shows that strategy and realistic expectations
(step 2) depend entirely on understanding the current market — yet professionals start
searches blind, without data on demand, layoffs, or salary benchmarks.

## Context
Starting a job search without market intelligence leads to miscalibrated expectations,
wasted effort, and poor timing decisions. Knowing the market is healthy or contracting
changes every downstream decision: urgency, salary targets, target companies, geography.

## Success looks like
- A professional can look at one view and know whether now is a good or bad time to search
- They can identify which roles and skills are in demand vs. declining
- They can set a realistic salary target before applying to anything
- They spend less than 5 minutes to get a clear market read
- They can see the trends clearly, how the hiring market numbers evolve through time

## Out of scope
- Application tracking or job pipeline management
- Resume or portfolio advice
- Company-specific research
